Cajun Shrimp Pasta

Back to main page

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 pound raw shrimp deveined, cleaned, tail removed
  • 2 tablespoons cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add salt to pot of water and bring to a boil. Cook pasta to al dente doneness. Before draining, scoop out at least 1/2 cup of the cooking water. Drain pasta and set aside.
  2. While pasta is cooking, add shrimp to a bowl with the cajun seasoning and olive oil and toss to combine
  3. Heat large skillet over medium high heat and add butter to melt. Add shrimp in a single layer and cook until golden brown, turning only once. Cook in multiple batches if needed
  4. Transfer shrimp to a clean plate or bowl and reduce heat to medium low
  5. Add heavy cream to pan and scrape bottom to release any stuck food, then wait for cream to bubble
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in parmesan cheese to melt.
  7. Add pasta and shrimp and stir to coat
  8. If needed to be thinned, and pasta water one table spoon at a time until desired consistency is met.
